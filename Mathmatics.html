What are the Subjects of the Scientific Math Section?

The Math and Science track is one of the main sections in high school. It focuses on subjects like physics, chemistry, and advanced mathematics. This track is perfect for students who love solving problems and thinking logically.

Chemistry

Chemistry is the science that studies matter, its properties, and how it changes. It explains how substances react with each other and how new materials are formed. Chemistry is used in medicine, food, energy, and everyday life.

Dynamics

Dynamics is a branch of mechanics that studies the motion of objects and the forces that cause them. It's crucial for understanding how things move, from planets in orbit to cars on a road. This field utilizes principles like Newton's laws of motion to predict and analyze the behavior of systems under various influences.

Physics

Calculus

Differentiation, a core concept in calculus, deals with the rate at which a quantity changes. It's fundamental for understanding slopes of curves, optimization problems, and how functions behave. This mathematical tool is widely applied in fields like physics, engineering, and economics to model and analyze dynamic systems.

Statics is a fundamental branch of mechanics focused on objects at rest or in constant motion. It explores the equilibrium of forces acting on a body, ensuring there's no net force or torque. This field is essential for designing stable structures.
